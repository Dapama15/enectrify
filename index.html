The barrier

Why SMEs stay on gas

Electrification is technically ready and economically attractive. The barrier is financial and operational — not technological.

Current situation

Gas-based process heat

  • ✕High, volatile energy costs tied to gas market prices
  • ✕Significant CO₂ emissions and growing carbon liabilities
  • ✕10–15 year payback — far beyond the 3-year SME threshold
  • ✕DKK 10M+ upfront CAPEX locked in non-core infrastructure
  • ✕Requires full-time engineering hires to manage internally
  • ✕Boilers run 24/7 — even when production is paused
